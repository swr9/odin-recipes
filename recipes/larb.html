Larb

Ground chicken larb

This 30-minute larb is crunchy, salty, spicy, and everything we want to eat tonight.

Ingredients

  • 1 red onion
  • 2 lbs ground chicken
  • rice vinegar
  • vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 scallions
  • 2 chiles
  • salt and pepper
  • soy sauce
  • fish sauce
  • sambal oelek
  • brown sugar
  • rice
  • lettuce
  • lime
  • cilantro
  • basil
  • mint

Steps

  1. First, thinly slice half a red onion and place in a small bowl with a few tablespoons of rice vinegar. Let it hang out in the fridge until you're ready to eat. Meanwhile, steam some white rice.
  2. Next, the chicken. Heat a slick of vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat until shimmering. Add a pound of ground chicken, two minced garlic cloves, three thinly sliced scallions (whites and greens), and a couple of sliced chiles. I like to use fiery Thai birds eye chiles, but jalape√±o, serrano, or Fresno would also work. Season with a little salt and pepper and cook until chicken is cooked through and browned. Stir in two tablespoons of soy sauce, a teaspoon of fish sauce, a small spoonful of sambal oelek or other hot sauce (like sriracha), and a tablespoon of brown sugar, cooking until the liquid is almost completely reduced.
  3. Serve the larb with rice, Bibb or butter lettuce leaves, lime wedges, quick-pickled onions, more sambal oelek, and torn herbs like cilantro, basil, and mint.
